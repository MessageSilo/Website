Cleanup the mess of messages

A platform to monitor and correct your dead-lettered messages

Azure Service Bus
RabbitMQ
AWS SQS

Created for developers who need to handle special cases of dead-lettered messages in different message-brokers

Monitor

Get notified when something goes wrong in your message queues.

CI/CD ready

Full control by our CLI called siloctl. Manage your connections in YAML files.

Free

Up to 5 connections in the free tier.
Try it now!

Correct your dead-lettered messages

You can define a custom JavaScript function that is called for every dead-letter message. Transform the message and re-enqueue it automatically!

siloctl apply -f myconn.yaml

From everything to everything

Pass messages between different queues and even message broker platforms!

Integrate your own APIs

Send the corrected messages to your own endpoints, or enrich the message with your own business logic!

siloctl apply -f myconn.yaml
